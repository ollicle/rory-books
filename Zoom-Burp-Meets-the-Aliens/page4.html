Burp checked his house on the hill and he checked all his house and all down his hill. He checked his backyard for the keys. Then he decided to give up on finding the keys for now. He could find those later.

So he thought perhaps the fireman had taken his dad, but he thought that was pretty unlikely. He decided to sneak out his back door, but when he did his path blocked by a bunch of weird creatures.

He said, “What the heck!” and quickly jumped over the fence.
